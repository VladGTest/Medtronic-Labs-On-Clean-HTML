Data Privacy & Security
We build technology that prioritizes safety, privacy, and security. Our Spice platform complies with all major international standards including GDPR, HIPPAA, and all regional regulations.
All data is encrypted at rest and in transit
All data used for reporting is deidentified before landing in our secure data warehouse
Access is restricted to data sets through role-based access and access is audited
All controls are in place and regular assessments on our products are performed against the latest privacy and security standards
